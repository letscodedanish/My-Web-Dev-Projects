Include HPV Vaccine in the Universal Immunization Programme #BeatCervicalCancer

HPV is a group of viruses that are known to cause a sexually transmitted infection, that can sometimes progress into cervical cancer, which is the most common cancer in rural Indian women and the second-most common cancer in urban Indian women. It is diagnosed so late that India has the highest rate of deaths from cervical cancer in the world. The HPV vaccine is very effective but is expensive and inaccessible to most of the country.
