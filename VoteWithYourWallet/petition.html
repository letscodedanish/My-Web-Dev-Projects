No Parking Lots under Parks in Mumbai- Pushpa.

The BMC has planned to push for underground parking facilities under the decades old public parks namely Pushpa Narsee Park in Juhu and Patwardhan Park in Bandra. This is being done under a current provision in the city's Development Control Regulations. Parks are the few remaining open spaces in Mumbai which has one of the poorest open spaces per capita ratios anywhere in the world.
